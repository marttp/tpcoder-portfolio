The voice AI revolution is transforming how businesses communicate. From automated customer service to AI-powered sales calls, voice agents are becoming essential tools across virtually every industry. This market map provides a comprehensive view of the B2B voice agents landscape.

## What Are Voice Agents?

Voice agents are AI-powered systems that can conduct natural conversations over phone calls, replacing or augmenting human agents. Unlike simple IVR systems, modern voice agents leverage large language models (LLMs) to understand context, respond naturally, and handle complex interactions.

## Market Overview

The B2B voice agents market has exploded with specialized solutions targeting specific verticals. Let's explore the key categories:

### Home Services

Companies like **Rosie**, **Goodcall**, **Sameday AI**, and **AVOCA** are revolutionizing how home service businesses handle incoming calls. These solutions help plumbers, electricians, and contractors never miss a lead while providing 24/7 availability.

### Restaurants

The restaurant industry has seen significant adoption with players like **Slang**, **Loman.ai**, **Kea**, and **Presto VOX** handling reservations, takeout orders, and customer inquiries. These systems integrate with POS systems and can manage high call volumes during peak hours.

### Recruiting & HR

AI voice agents are transforming talent acquisition. **ALPHARUN**, **Apriora**, **WAYFASTER**, and **heyMilo** conduct initial candidate screenings, schedule interviews, and assess qualifications at scale. This category also includes **Tengai** and **Purplefish** for unbiased interview processes.

### Healthcare

Healthcare represents one of the largest segments, divided into three sub-categories:

**Front Office**: **Simbie**, **AssortHealth**, **Clarion**, and **Hyro** handle patient scheduling, appointment reminders, and general inquiries.

**Back Office**: **Infinitus**, **Tennr**, and **SuperDial** automate insurance verification, prior authorizations, and administrative calls that traditionally consumed hours of staff time.

**Patient Management**: **Hippocratic AI** and **Pear** focus on patient engagement and care coordination.

### Finance & Collections

**Skit.ai**, **CollectWise**, **Domu**, and **Contour** are tackling debt collection and financial services, where compliance and tone are critical. These solutions can handle sensitive conversations while maintaining regulatory compliance.

### Sales

The sales category includes **11x**, **Air**, **Alta**, and **Ello** - AI SDRs that can conduct outbound calls, qualify leads, and book meetings. **ARTISAN** offers AI employees that integrate into sales workflows.

### Customer Service

This is perhaps the most mature category with established players like **PolyAI**, **Replicant**, **Ada**, and **CRESTA**. Newer entrants include **Yellow.ai**, **Parloa**, and **SIERRA** (from the founders of Uber). These platforms handle millions of customer interactions across various industries.

### Horizontal Platforms

Infrastructure providers like **ElevenLabs**, **VAPI**, **Retell AI**, and **SoundHound** offer the building blocks for developers to create custom voice agents. **Synthflow** and **BLAND AI** provide no-code solutions for businesses to deploy voice agents quickly.

## Emerging Verticals

Several specialized verticals are gaining traction:

- **Auto Dealers**: **Toma**, **Numa**, and **STELLA** handle service appointments and sales inquiries
- **Real Estate**: **Liberate**, **Sonant**, and **FURTHER AI** qualify leads and schedule showings
- **Legal**: **Caseflood.ai**, **Legal27**, and **Riviera** manage client intake
- **Logistics**: **HappyRobot** and **FleetWorks** coordinate with drivers and dispatchers
- **Government**: **PREPARED** and **HYPER** assist with emergency services and citizen engagement

## Key Trends

### 1. Vertical Specialization
Rather than one-size-fits-all solutions, we're seeing deep vertical specialization. A voice agent for healthcare needs different compliance features than one for restaurants.

### 2. Integration Depth
Success depends on integrating with existing systems - CRMs, EHRs, POS systems, and scheduling tools. The best solutions feel like natural extensions of existing workflows.

### 3. Human-AI Collaboration
Most solutions aren't about replacing humans entirely but augmenting them. AI handles routine calls while complex issues escalate to human agents.

## Conclusion

The B2B voice agents market is experiencing rapid growth and consolidation. As LLM technology improves and costs decrease, we'll likely see voice agents become standard infrastructure for businesses of all sizes. The companies that succeed will be those that deeply understand their target verticals and deliver measurable ROI through automation.

Whether you're a startup looking to scale customer service or an enterprise seeking to modernize operations, there's likely a voice agent solution tailored to your industry. The question is no longer whether to adopt voice AI, but which solution best fits your needs.
